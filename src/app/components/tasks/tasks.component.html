How it works

1 . Sign up for a WeRecycle account or log in if you're an existing user. For Recycling companies contact admin for your company to be added in the system and connect to your clients easily. 2. Search for Recycling companies in your area by specifying your location and materials to recycled. 3. Contact the recycling company directly or send a recycling request through the app. 4. Once a request is approved, arrange for items to be recycled and visit convenient location at specified time. 5. After the recycling, both the company and user can update their request status on the app.
